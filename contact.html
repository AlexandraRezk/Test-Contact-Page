Marisa Cohen

Creator of Glitch Witch, Character Designer, Writer, Storyboarder, UX Designer

I create stories, characters and animations; otherwise known as the socially acceptable way for grownups to have imaginary friends. I’m an animator and designer, with degrees in Computer Animation/Interactive Media and Illustration from the Fashion Institute of Technology.

I first came up with Glitch Witch during my last year of college for a class assignment...despite the fact that it had nothing to do with the assignment. The project was to design anything interactive and creative in one semester. This somehow inspired me to come up with a character who merges interactive technology, with magic--and I made it into an interactive webcomic. My classmates all very enthusiastically told me that I should continue the story for Bex. My goal now is to make more webcomics, web games, and animations for Glitch Witch.

Alexandra Rezk

Programmer, Web Developer

I speak the secret language of computers, to code digital magic with just strokes of a keyboard; I'm a programmer. I graduated from New York Institute of technology with a Bachelors in Computer Engineering and I am looking to be a Web Developer.

Marisa told me about her Glitch Witch story and I thought it was a really good idea. When she asked me to help her create her website I was very interested and excited. I’ve converted Marisa’s UX Designs into a website using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery. I also created all the games on the site using Phaser. I look forward to maintaining the site and creating new websites.
